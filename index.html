Meet Abi Simmons

Clinical Social Work / Therapist, LMSW

Hi there! I'm Abi Simmons.

I believe meaningful change happens in the context of safe, supportive relationships. At Simply Restored, my work is grounded in helping individuals, couples, and families better understand themselves and each other, especially when life feels overwhelming, disconnected, or stuck.

My approach is relational, collaborative, and thoughtful. Rather than focusing on blame or quick fixes, I help clients slow down, recognize patterns, and build insight that leads to healthier communication and stronger connection. I strive to create a calm, nonjudgmental space where people feel heard, respected, and supported.
